Give Food

We're a UK charity that delivers food & supplies to food banks - using technology to supply, in bulk, what's required in a timely manner.

We've delivered {{ total_weight|floatformat:2 }} tonnes of food, consisting of {{ total_items|intcomma }} items and {{ total_calories|floatformat:2 }} million calories, to {{ no_active_foodbanks }} food banks around the country.

Whilst we believe that food banks should not exist in our country, we aim to help local organisations address the immediate and critical need created by food insecurity.

We operate independent of religion and are privately funded by the Cartwright family.

Order

An order is created when a need is published. This usually happens within an hour or two, but our record is just four minutes! The delivery is made anywhere between 12 hours and a week later.

Feedback

We get feedback to make sure we've ordered the correct items for delivery at the right time to the best location.

What we deliver

We find that items like baked beans, tinned spaghetti and dried pasta are often well provisioned at food banks already.

As such, we focus on delivering tinned and heavy food like tomatoes, vegetables, meat, fish, fruit, custard, and rice pudding plus UHT milk. Occasionally we'll also supply hygiene products like shower gel, deodorant and shampoo.

We only deliver items that are requested, and suitable for storage on a shelf for extended periods.

Where we deliver

We currently supply {{ no_active_foodbanks }} food banks across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland of varying sizes. Some are members of networks and some are independent.
