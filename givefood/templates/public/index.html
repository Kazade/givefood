We automatically monitor food bank shopping lists & social media thousands of times a day to identify what a location needs.

Order

An order is created when a need is published. This usually happens within an hour or two, but our record is just six minutes! The delivery is made anywhere between 12 hours and a week later.

Feedback

We get feedback to make sure we've ordered the correct items for delivery at the right time to the best location.

What we deliver

We find that items like baked beans, tinned spaghetti and dried pasta are often well provisioned at food banks already.

As such, we focus on delivering tinned and heavy food like tomatoes, vegetables, meat, fish, fruit, custard, and rice pudding plus UHT milk. Occasionally we'll also supply hygiene products like shower gel, deodorant and shampoo.

We only deliver items suitable for storage on a shelf for extended periods.

Where we deliver

We currently supply {{ no_foodbanks }} food banks across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland of varying sizes. Some are members of networks and some are independent.
